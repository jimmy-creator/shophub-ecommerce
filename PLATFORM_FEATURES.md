## Everything You Need to Sell Online

### 1. Beautiful Storefront

Your store looks professional from day one.

- **6 Premium Themes** — Switch between Warm Luxury, Midnight Dark, Clean Minimal, Forest Green, Royal Blue, and Modern Marketplace with one click
- **Mobile-First Design** — Over 70% of online shopping happens on phones. Your store looks perfect on every screen size
- **App-Like Mobile Experience** — Bottom navigation bar, smooth transitions, and touch-optimized buttons that feel native
- **Hero Banners** — Full-width promotional banners with your own images
- **Category Browsing** — Visual category icons with custom images

### 2. Product Management

Add and manage products with ease — no technical knowledge needed.

- **Rich Product Pages** — Multiple images with gallery view, detailed descriptions, brand, and pricing
- **Product Variants** — Sell the same product in different sizes, colors, or any combination. Each variant can have its own price and stock
- **Compare Pricing** — Show original price with strikethrough and sale price to highlight discounts
- **Bulk Import/Export** — Upload hundreds of products at once using a simple CSV spreadsheet. Download all products to edit in Excel
- **Smart Search** — Customers find products instantly with autocomplete suggestions as they type
- **Category Management** — Organize products into categories with custom images
- **Related Products** — "You May Also Like" section automatically shows similar products
- **Recently Viewed** — Customers can easily find products they looked at before

### 3. Shopping Experience

Every detail is designed to convert visitors into buyers.

- **Guest Checkout** — Customers can buy without creating an account. No friction, more sales
- **Wishlist** — Save products for later with a heart button. Comes back to buy when ready
- **Product Reviews** — Real customer reviews with star ratings build trust and increase sales
- **Buy Now Button** — Skip the cart and go straight to checkout for impulse purchases
- **Pincode Checker** — Customers check delivery availability before buying. Reduces failed deliveries
- **Multiple Views** — Grid, two-column, or list view — customers browse the way they prefer

### 4. Payments That Work in India

Accept every payment method your customers use.

- **Razorpay** — Cards, UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe, net banking, wallets — all in one
- **Paytm** — India's most trusted payment brand
- **Cash on Delivery** — Still the #1 payment method in India. Built right in
- **Bank Transfer** — For customers who prefer direct transfer
- **Secure Payments** — Every transaction is encrypted and verified server-side. No fraud
- **Automatic Payment Verification** — Orders are confirmed only after payment is verified with the gateway

### 5. Order Management

Track every order from placement to delivery.

- **Real-Time Order Tracking** — Processing → Confirmed → Shipped → Delivered
- **Order Cancellation** — Customers can cancel orders before shipping. Stock is automatically restored
- **Automatic Refunds** — When orders are cancelled, refunds are initiated automatically
- **Invoice PDF** — Professional GST-compliant invoices generated automatically for every order. Download or email
- **Order History** — Customers see all their orders with status, items, and amounts
- **Guest Order Tracking** — Even guests can track orders using their email and order number

### 6. Discounts & Promotions

Drive sales with powerful promotional tools.

- **Coupon Codes** — Create percentage or fixed amount discount codes
- **Usage Limits** — Set how many times a coupon can be used, total and per customer
- **Minimum Order Amount** — Require minimum cart value for coupon eligibility
- **Date Scheduling** — Set start and end dates for time-limited promotions
- **Category-Specific Coupons** — Target discounts to specific product categories
- **Max Discount Cap** — Set a ceiling on percentage discounts to protect margins

### 7. Shipping

Flexible shipping that fits your business.

- **Standard & Express Options** — Customers choose between economy and fast delivery
- **Free Shipping Threshold** — Encourage larger orders with "Free shipping above ₹500"
- **Configurable Rates** — Set flat rates, per-item charges, and express premiums
- **Pincode-Based Delivery** — Control exactly which areas you deliver to
- **Delivery Time Estimates** — Show estimated delivery days based on pincode
- **COD Availability** — Enable or disable Cash on Delivery per pincode

### 8. Tax Compliance (GST)

Built for Indian tax requirements from the ground up.

- **GST Inclusive Pricing** — Prices include tax, just like Amazon and Flipkart. No surprises at checkout
- **Automatic CGST/SGST vs IGST** — System automatically splits tax based on whether the customer is in the same state or different state
- **5 GST Slabs** — Support for 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28% rates
- **HSN Codes** — Add HSN codes to products for GST filing compliance
- **Tax on Invoices** — Every invoice shows the complete tax breakdown
- **Per-Product Tax Control** — Choose which products are taxable and at what rate

### 9. Customer Communication

Stay connected with your customers automatically.

- **Order Confirmation Emails** — Sent automatically when an order is placed
- **Payment Confirmation** — Sent when online payment is verified
- **Shipping Updates** — Customers are emailed when order status changes
- **Abandoned Cart Recovery** — If a customer adds items to cart but doesn't buy, they receive a reminder email after 1 hour with their saved items
- **Password Reset Emails** — Secure password recovery flow
- **Professional Email Templates** — All emails are beautifully designed with your store branding

### 10. Admin Dashboard

Everything you need to run your business, in one place.

- **Revenue Analytics** — Total revenue, monthly revenue, daily revenue with growth percentage
- **Sales Charts** — Visual bar charts showing revenue trends over 30 days or 12 months
- **Top Products** — See which products are selling the most
- **Order Status Breakdown** — Visual breakdown of processing, shipped, delivered orders
- **Customer Management** — View all registered and guest customers with order history and total spent
- **Recent Orders** — Quick view of latest orders with payment status
- **Payment Method Stats** — See which payment methods customers prefer
- **Inventory Alerts** — Real-time list of out-of-stock and low-stock products with email notifications
- **Low Stock Email Alerts** — Automatic daily email when products run low

### 11. Security

Your store and customer data are protected.

- **Encrypted Passwords** — Industry-standard bcrypt hashing
- **JWT Authentication** — Secure token-based login with httpOnly cookies
- **XSS Protection** — All user inputs are sanitized to prevent script injection
- **Rate Limiting** — Protection against brute force attacks on login and APIs
- **HTTPS Enforced** — All traffic encrypted with SSL
- **Secure Payment Verification** — Payments verified server-side, never trusting client data
- **Password Strength Rules** — Minimum 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and numbers

### 12. Team Management

Grow your team without giving away full control.

- **Staff Accounts** — Create separate logins for your team members
- **Granular Permissions** — Choose exactly what each staff member can access: Products, Orders, Coupons, Reviews, Customers, Analytics, Categories, or Settings
- **Admin vs Staff** — Only the main admin can create or remove staff accounts
- **Activity Separation** — Each person logs in with their own credentials

### 13. SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

Get found on Google without hiring an SEO expert.

- **Dynamic Meta Tags** — Every page has proper title, description, and Open Graph tags for social sharing
- **Product SEO** — Product pages automatically include price, availability, and image in meta tags
- **Sitemap.xml** — Auto-generated sitemap with all products, categories, and pages. Google can crawl your entire store
- **robots.txt** — Properly configured to guide search engines to the right pages
- **Canonical URLs** — Prevent duplicate content issues

### 14. Google Login

Make registration effortless for customers.

- **One-Click Signup** — Customers sign up or log in with their Google account
- **No Password Needed** — Google handles authentication securely
- **Automatic Account Creation** — First-time Google users get an account created automatically
